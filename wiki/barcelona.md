# Barcelona

FC Barcelona is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Globally famous for their youth academy La Masia and positional play principles, the club pioneered the Tiki-Taka tactical system under Pep Guardiola, creating one of the most dominant possession teams in football history.
