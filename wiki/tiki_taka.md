# Tiki Taka

Tiki-Taka is a style of play in football characterized by short passing, high possession, and fluid movement. It is heavily associated with Pep Guardiola's Barcelona and the Spain national team, aiming to control the tempo of the game, tire out opponents, and create openings through constant triangular passes.
