# Jude Bellingham

Jude Bellingham is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Real Madrid and the England national team. An dynamic box-to-box midfielder with exceptional ball-carrying, defensive work rate, and late box arrivals, he transitioned into a high-scoring False Nine role during his debut season in Spain, winning La Liga.
