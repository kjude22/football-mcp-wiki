# Paris Saint Germain

Paris Saint-Germain is a professional football club based in Paris, France. Competing in Ligue 1, they are France's most successful modern club, famous for assembling world-class attacking lines featuring global stars like Kylian Mbappé, Neymar Jr, and Lionel Messi.
