# Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward. Having spent the majority of his career at Barcelona, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Messi is famous for his dribbling close control, playmaking vision, and prolific goalscoring records, frequently dropping deep to play as a False Nine in Pep Guardiola's legendary Barcelona setup.
