# Manchester City

Manchester City is an English football club based in Manchester, competing in the Premier League. Guided by manager Pep Guardiola, the team has dominated modern English football using high-possession positional concepts, utilizing playmakers like Kevin De Bruyne and elite finishers like Erling Haaland.
