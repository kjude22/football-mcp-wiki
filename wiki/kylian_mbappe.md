# Kylian Mbappe

Kylian Mbappé is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Real Madrid and the France national team. Renowned for his explosive acceleration, dribbling skills, and clinical finishing, he became the all-time top scorer for Paris Saint-Germain before his high-profile transfer to Real Madrid in 2024.
