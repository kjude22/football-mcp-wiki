# Bayern Munich

FC Bayern Munich is a German professional sports club based in Munich, Bavaria. The dominant force in German football, the club relies on high-intensity pressing and wing-focused attacking systems, having secured multiple historic European trebles and signing star striker Harry Kane in 2023.
