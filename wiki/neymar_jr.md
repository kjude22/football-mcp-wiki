# Neymar Jr

Neymar Jr is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a winger and playmaker. Famous for his creative flair, dribbling skills, and playmaking improvisation, he formed the iconic 'MSN' attacking trio at Barcelona alongside Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez, winning the historic continental treble in 2015.
