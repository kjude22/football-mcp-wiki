# Liverpool

Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. Under Jürgen Klopp, the club became globally famous for its intense, full-throttle Gegenpressing football style, relying on high-line pressing, overlapping fullbacks, and rapid transitions to capture domestic and European titles.
