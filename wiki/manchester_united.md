# Manchester United

Manchester United is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. One of the most supported and successful clubs globally, the club achieved legendary status under Sir Alex Ferguson, known for dynamic wing play, tactical flexibility, and famous comebacks.
