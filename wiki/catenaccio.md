# Catenaccio

Catenaccio is a tactical system in football with a strong emphasis on defense. Originating in Switzerland and refined in Italy, it utilizes a defensive line with a sweeper (libero) behind the defenders, aiming to nullify opponent attacks and secure narrow victories via structured counter-attacks.
