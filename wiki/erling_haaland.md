# Erling Haaland

Erling Haaland is a Norwegian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Premier League club Manchester City. He is widely considered one of the best strikers in world football due to his raw pace, physical strength, positioning, and clinical box finishing, breaking the Premier League single-season scoring record in his debut year.
