# Gegenpressing

Gegenpressing (German for counter-pressing) is a tactical philosophy where a team, upon losing possession, immediately attempts to win the ball back rather than falling back into a defensive shape. Popularized by managers like Jurgen Klopp, it restricts opponent transition spaces and triggers instant offensive moves.
