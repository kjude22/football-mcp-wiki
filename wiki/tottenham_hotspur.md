# Tottenham Hotspur

Tottenham Hotspur is an English professional football club based in London that competes in the Premier League. Spurs are known for playing offensive, high-pressing football, historically utilizing variations of Gegenpressing to recover possession in advanced zones, captained by South Korea's Son Heung-min.
