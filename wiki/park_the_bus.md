# Park the Bus

Park the Bus is a colloquial term describing an extremely defensive low-block tactical setup where almost all outfield players remain behind the ball deep in their own half. Made famous by managers like Jose Mourinho at Chelsea, it aims to shut down all space, frustrate high-scoring opponents, and defend a lead.
