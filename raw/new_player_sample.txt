Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. He won five Ballon d'Or awards and became Real Madrid's all-time leading goal scorer. Ronaldo is known for his unmatched aerial ability, athleticism, physical power, and clutch goalscoring in the UEFA Champions League, dominating English, Spanish, and Italian football. He is currently playing for Al-Nassr.
